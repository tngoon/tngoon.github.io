Using CritiqueKit

Welcome to CritiqueKit! CritiqueKit aims to help you provide better feedback by providing you with feedback suggestions and real-time categorization of your feedback. To get started, begin typing your feedback in the text box.

  • The checkboxes will underneath the textbox will check as you type depending on whether your feedback fits the characteristics of specific, actionable, and justified. Hover over each checkbox to learn more about what each of these characteristics means.
  • If you need help with thinking of something to provide feedback on, the suggestions box can give you some inspiration with previously used feedback. Select the suggestion to copy it directly into the textbox and continue editing your comment.
